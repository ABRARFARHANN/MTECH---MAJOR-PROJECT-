Overview

What this project does

Carelytics is a research prototype that combines multiple models to help you understand health risks from everyday data:

  • Mental health risk from sleep, activity, and text sentiment.
  • Hypertension risk from blood pressure and lifestyle indicators.
  • Diabetes risk from metabolic and clinical measurements.

Designed for learning and exploration — not for diagnosis or medical decision making.
