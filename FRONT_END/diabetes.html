Diabetes model

How this model uses your data

This diabetes model uses standard clinical measurements to estimate an overall diabetes risk level based on today's values.

  • Metabolic indicators such as fasting glucose and BMI.
  • Physiological measures including blood pressure and skin thickness.
  • Longer-term risk factors: insulin, age, and diabetes pedigree.

Designed for learning and exploration — not for diagnosis or medical decision making.
