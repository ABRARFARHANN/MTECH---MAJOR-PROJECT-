Blood pressure model

How this model uses your data

This model combines simple demographic and vital-sign information to estimate the risk of high blood pressure.

  • Core demographics such as age and biological sex.
  • Body composition through height, weight, and derived BMI.
  • Resting heart rate as a proxy for cardiovascular strain.

Designed for learning and exploration — not for diagnosis or medical decision making.
