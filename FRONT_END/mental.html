Mental health model

How this model uses your data

This mental health model combines several signals from the same day to estimate an overall daily mental health risk level.

  • Night-time sleep patterns: time in bed, total sleep, awakenings, and WASO.
  • Daytime activity and mobility: steps, walking, time sitting, and social contact.
  • Short text notes or messages capturing how you felt during the day.

Designed for learning and exploration — not for diagnosis or medical decision making.
